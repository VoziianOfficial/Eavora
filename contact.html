After you submit

What happens after your request is sent?

Eavora keeps the next steps simple and transparent. The provider relationship, if any, is handled directly between you and the provider.

01

Your request is received.

Your submitted details are received through the contact endpoint and used to understand the request category.

02

Details may be reviewed for matching.

The category, message, service area, and contact details may help route the request more clearly.

03

Providers may contact you.

Participating providers may contact you where available with provider-supplied options or questions.

04

You decide whether to continue.

You can review details, ask questions, and choose whether a provider option fits your needs.
