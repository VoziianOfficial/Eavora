This page is provided for general website transparency and should be reviewed for your specific business, location, and legal requirements before launch.

1. Overview

Eavora operates as an independent provider-matching platform that helps homeowners submit gutter-related request details and compare available local provider options where available. Eavora does not perform gutter services directly.

By using this website or submitting a request form, you acknowledge that information you provide may be processed as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily submit through the website, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Selected gutter service category
  • Message or project details you choose to provide
  • Consent confirmation submitted through the form

We may also collect basic technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referral source, and interaction data used to maintain website functionality and improve user experience.

3. How We Use Information

Eavora may use submitted information to:

  • Receive and process gutter-related requests
  • Understand the selected service category and request details
  • Communicate with users about submitted requests
  • Connect users with participating independent providers where available
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the website
  • Prevent spam, misuse, fraudulent submissions, or technical abuse
  • Comply with applicable obligations and enforce website terms

4. How Information May Be Shared

When you submit a request, Eavora may share relevant request details with participating independent providers so they may contact you directly regarding provider-supplied options, availability, pricing approach, scheduling, scope, or service terms.

Eavora may also share information with service providers that help operate the website, process form submissions, host website files, manage security, or support communications. These parties may only access information as needed to provide their services.

Eavora may disclose information if required to comply with legal obligations, protect website security, prevent fraud, respond to lawful requests, or protect the rights, safety, and property of Eavora, users, or others.

5. Independent Providers

Providers are independent from Eavora. If you choose to continue with a provider, that provider may collect additional information under its own privacy practices and service terms. Eavora is not responsible for the privacy practices, service terms, pricing, scheduling, warranties, licensing, insurance, or work performed by independent providers.

6. Your Choices

You may choose not to submit a request through the website. If you have already submitted information and want to request access, correction, or deletion, you may contact Eavora using the contact details listed below.

Depending on your location, you may have additional privacy rights. Eavora will review reasonable privacy-related requests as required by applicable law.

7. Security & Retention

Eavora uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to help protect submitted information. However, no website, transmission method, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to process requests, operate the website, maintain records, resolve disputes, prevent misuse, and comply with applicable obligations.

8. Cookies & Tracking

This website may use cookies or similar technologies for essential functionality, user experience, analytics, performance, and security. For more information, please review the Cookie Policy.
