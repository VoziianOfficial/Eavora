Why Eavora exists

Most gutter requests begin with uncertainty.

A homeowner may notice overflow, leaking joints, clogged gutters, drainage issues, or aging materials without knowing which category fits best. Eavora gives that first step a cleaner structure.

Instead of making direct service claims, Eavora helps homeowners describe the request, choose a likely category, and compare provider-supplied options where available.

Eavora does not perform gutter work directly. Participating providers are independent, and homeowners choose whether to continue.
